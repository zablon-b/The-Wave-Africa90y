VISION

To see a vibrant, self-reliant generation of African youth who are spiritually grounded, socially responsible and empowered to lead with integrity.

WHO WE ARE

The Wave Africa is a Christian-led youth movement located in Changamwe, Mombasa.

We are on a mission dedicated to spiritual renewal and social empowerment for the youth of Africa, anchoring the next generation in faith while equipping them with the tools to lead with integrity.

We aim to restore ancient landmarks that point to the values and purposes God established for generations.

Led by a team striving with heartfelt integrity, we're preparing today's youth with the tools to lead the world of tomorrow, equipping them with integrity.
