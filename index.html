Who We Are

A Movement of Purpose

The Wave Africa is a Christian-led youth group based in Changamwe, Mombasa. We are a movement dedicated to spiritual renewal and social empowerment for the youth of Africa.

We believe in anchoring the next generation in faith while equipping them with the tools to lead with integrity.

Through music, mentorship, and community action, we are raising a generation that stands as a wave of change — restoring the moral, spiritual, and social foundations of our communities.

What We Do

Key Programs

The Worshippers Affair

Major spiritual gatherings for worship and renewal. These events bring together hundreds of youth in powerful worship experiences that reignite faith and purpose.

Landmark Praise

High-impact events celebrating gospel talent and ministry. A flagship platform that showcases and nurtures musical gifts while glorifying God through the arts.

Talent Mentorship

Professional coaching for musicians, artists, and creative ministers. We invest in raw talent with structured mentorship, transforming potential into purposeful ministry.

Youth Advocacy

Programs steering youth away from social vices toward productive lives. We create safe spaces, alternative pathways, and accountability networks for vulnerable young people.
